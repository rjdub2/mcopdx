About

Owner Margaux Wagenmann has extensive experience in the Oregon wine industry & is eager to share her love of wine. She has hand picked an outstanding group of sommeliers & wine professionals who share her passion for wine and educating consumers. All have 5-20+ years experience in the wine industry, specializing in consumer relations.

Contact us when you need a Professional Pourer to represent and sell your wines at a local wine shop, retailer, special event or even as fill-in support for your tasting room.

In addition to fill-in pourers, Margaux can contact retailers & wine shops in the Portland Metro area and coordinate wine demos on your behalf. Only a Professional Pourer with knowledge of your wine will be sent to pour at select retailers & wine shops. Examples include New Seasons Markets, Zupan’s, Mom & Pop Wine Shop, Wizer’s, Woodstock Wine & Deli and many, many more!

Our goal is to spread our love of wines to consumers - to educate & make a lasting positive impression.
